About me

Vanessa Bilodeau

(Hover over this to see a cute tiger cub )

My name is Vanessa Bilodeau and I am the creator of this website. I am a freshman at UNH Manchester! I am majoring in Psychology. I have always loved tigers ever since I was little. I based my inspiration for this website from the NWF's website. There is a endangerment crisis going on with tigers. This website is here to educate others and make them aware of the issues at hand. Feel free to donate to the charity on this website! Save these Tigers!
